Fire Development Services Enhancement

The department is enhancing its Fire Development Services to improve fire and life safety systems across the city. This involves reviewing over 10,000 building plans annually, conducting pre-development meetings, and performing field inspections for new constructions and remodels. The goal is to ensure compliance with fire safety regulations and to streamline the approval process for developers.
