About Operation USA

Operation USA is a Los Angeles-based international disaster relief and development agency that helps communities at home and abroad overcome the effects of disasters, disease, violence, and endemic poverty.

The organization prides itself on being a hands-on agency with a small, dedicated staff that directly oversees its projects and disaster response work. Unburdened by the bureaucracy and overhead of larger organizations, Operation USA deploys creative solutions to meet real needs in real time.

Founding Board member Julie Andrews once described Operation USA as “the little ship that gets in the harbor where the big ships cannot go.” This philosophy reflects the organization’s steadfast commitment to navigating challenging environments and reaching communities in need, no matter how inaccessible they may be.
