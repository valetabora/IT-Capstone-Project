Who We Are

Phoenix Foundation is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to helping communities rebuild after devastating wildfires. We connect donors with verified charities and provide a secure platform for disaster relief donations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support individuals and families affected by wildfires by providing a transparent and efficient donation platform. We ensure that every dollar goes toward trusted relief efforts, helping communities recover and rebuild.
